Safety Guide
Dubai remains safe and fully operational.
Emergency alerts may occasionally be issued as part of the UAE’s national early warning system. The following guidance helps residents respond calmly and safely if an alert is received.
If You Receive an Emergency Alert
Seek Shelter Immediately
Move inside the nearest secure building and go to an interior room or corridor away from windows, balconies, and external doors.
Stay in a Safe Position
Remain away from glass walls or partitions. Sit against an interior wall and protect your head and neck.
Follow Instructions
Listen to building announcements and follow directions from security or authorities. Avoid elevators unless instructed and keep exits clear.
Wait for the “All Clear”
Remain indoors until an official notification is issued. Once cleared, resume activities calmly and check on family or neighbors if needed.
If You Are Outdoors
Enter the nearest building immediately and move to an interior space away from windows. Avoid open areas such as rooftops, balconies, parks, and beaches.
If You Are Driving
Continue driving safely to your destination or the nearest building. Park and move indoors as soon as possible.
If You See Debris or Suspicious Objects
Do not approach or touch debris. Move away and notify authorities.
Responsible Information Sharing
Avoid filming interceptions, photographing debris, or sharing unverified information online.
