Autism Awareness Day
Autism is a complex neuro-biological condition that affects the social interactions and communication skills of individuals. However, the biggest challenges for those affected by this disorder are prejudice and misinformation.
Wasl is proud to support annual autism awareness campaign, an initiative under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, that aims to support children with autism.
Let’s play our part as a thriving community and learn more about autism cases to help build a supportive community for everyone.
